2. Privacy-First Architecture

loveUAD is built on a privacy-first foundation:

  • No Personal Identification: We do NOT collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, postal addresses, or any personally identifiable information.
  • Anonymous Access: Users access the App using randomly generated 17-digit anonymous connection codes.
  • Zero-Knowledge Architecture: We cannot identify who you are or link your usage to your real identity.

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Information You Provide

  • Anonymous Connection Code: A 17-digit randomly generated code used to access the App (no personal information attached).
  • Chat Messages: Questions and descriptions you submit to receive dementia caregiving guidance (stored anonymously).
  • Care Context: Information about dementia symptoms, behaviors, and caregiving situations you describe (anonymous, not linked to any individual).

3.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, App version.
  • Usage Data: Features accessed, time spent in App, error logs (for technical support and App improvement).
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated, anonymous usage statistics to improve the App.

3.3 Information We DO NOT Collect

  • ✗ Real names or usernames
  • ✗ Email addresses
  • ✗ Phone numbers
  • ✗ Postal addresses
  • ✗ Precise geolocation data
  • ✗ Photos or videos
  • ✗ Financial information
  • ✗ Health records or medical diagnoses
  • ✗ Government-issued IDs

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the anonymous information collected for:

  • Providing AI-Powered Guidance: Processing your questions through AI models to provide dementia caregiving advice.
  • Service Improvement: Analyzing usage patterns to improve the App's functionality and user experience.
  • Technical Support: Diagnosing and fixing technical issues.
  • Security: Detecting and preventing fraudulent or unauthorized use.
  • Compliance: Meeting legal and regulatory obligations.
  • Research: Conducting aggregated, anonymous research to improve dementia care support (no individual identification possible).

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted and stored is encrypted using TLS 1.3 and AES-256 encryption.
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls limit who can access backend systems.
  • Regular Audits: Security audits and vulnerability assessments.
  • NHS-Compliant Infrastructure: Hosted on NHS Digital-approved cloud infrastructure (evaluation in progress).
  • No Cross-Referencing: Anonymous connection codes cannot be linked to individuals.
Note: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of data transmitted over the internet.

7. Data Retention

  • Chat History: Retained for service provision unless you request deletion.
  • Anonymous Usage Data: Retained for up to 2 years for analytics and improvement purposes.
  • Technical Logs: Retained for 90 days for security and debugging purposes.
  • Your Right to Delete: Contact us anytime to delete all data associated with your connection code.

8. Your Rights (GDPR/UK GDPR)

Even though we collect minimal anonymous data, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request access to data associated with your connection code.
  • Right to Deletion: Request deletion of all data linked to your connection code.
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request a copy of your data in machine-readable format.
  • Right to Object: Object to certain data processing activities.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time.

To exercise these rights: Email support@loveuad.com with your 17-digit connection code. We will respond within 30 days.
